The History of AI

Artificial Intelligence has a rich history that dates back to the mid-20th century. Here are some key milestones:

  • 1956: The term "Artificial Intelligence" was coined at the Dartmouth Conference.
  • 1970s: Expert systems were developed, which could perform specific tasks like diagnosing diseases.
  • 1980s: AI research faced funding cuts but saw a resurgence with the development of neural networks.
  • 1990s: Machine learning algorithms improved, leading to better speech recognition and image processing.
  • 2000s: AI became more integrated into everyday products, such as smartphones and personal assistants.
  • 2010s: Advances in deep learning and big data led to significant improvements in AI capabilities.
