Applications of AI

AI is used in various fields such as healthcare, finance, transportation, and more. Here are some examples:

Healthcare

AI is used for medical diagnosis, drug discovery, personalized treatment plans, and robotic surgery.

Finance

AI helps in fraud detection, algorithmic trading, customer service chatbots, and risk management.

Transportation

Self-driving cars, traffic management systems, and route optimization are some applications of AI in transportation.
